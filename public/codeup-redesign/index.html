Admissions Process

Application

Fill out our quick application here. No commitment at all! Once we receive your completed app, we'll reach out to you to schedule the next steps.

Interview

This 20-minute phone interview gives us a chance to get to know you a bit better. Also, you can ask us any questions you have about our program.

Tests

You'll take two 40-minute tests: first math, then logic. We look for students with strong problem-solving skills and an eagerness to work through challenge!

Decision

It's never fun to keep people waiting. After you complete the interview and tests, we'll update you on your acceptance status within 24 hours.

Our Story

Codeup’s story begins as many startups do: with a problem that needed solving.

In late 2013, Codeup’s three co-founders – Michael Girdley, Jason Straughan, and Chris Turner – noticed many of their fellow programmers and entrepreneurs were having a tough time finding quality web developers to grow their businesses. After some brainstorming and researching, the trio decided to open San Antonio’s first coding bootcamp with the mission of changing people’s lives through immersive web development education.


The three co-founders rounded up a dedicated group of instructors and staff members and created a space in San Antonio where people can learn programming in a supportive, creative environment.


The Codeup team hopes to make their corner of the world a little better by solving a big, meaningful problem for the community and the country — one person at a time.

Michael Girdley
CEO

Michael is a long-time entrepreneur and investor from San Antonio. His published works include 6 books on Java Programming with a focus on web applications. He holds a computer science degree with honors from Lafayette College. Michael has worked in enterprise software (BEA / WebLogic), security (RSA Security), venture capitalist (Geekdom Fund) and as CEO of Alamo Fireworks.

Jason Straughan
CTO

Jason is a long-time programmer, mentor and teacher. He also operates Grok Interactive, a team of software engineers with proven talent in Ruby on Rails, PHP, javascript, HTML, CSS, and most major databases. He is the author of numerous popular programming books including “Beginning Kohana 3 Development” and founder of San Antonio Web Developer Meetup.

Chris Turner
Director of Curriculum

Chris is an experienced innovator and entrepreneur from Orlando, Florida. He received his Bachelor of Science in Computer Engineering at the University of Central Florida. He worked as a technical lead and developer for Lockheed Martin for 12 years before leaving to form his own company. Chris is now CEO of Turner Logic, a technology consulting company.
